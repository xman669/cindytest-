WHO WE SERVE

Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT) is an important benefit for people who need assistance getting to and from medical appointments. At Natural Care Transportation, LLC., we provide (NEMT) services for Medicaid clients, including Children with Special Health Care Needs (CSHCN) and Transportation for Indigent Cancer Patients (TICP) and clients who have no other means of transportation.

HOW WILL WE SERVE

Natural Care will serve our clients with the highest appreciation, for bringing your services to us. We will bring exceptional quality services to you with our experienced team. You will experience a safe and professional service with trained and reliable drivers, who handles our Seniors and disabled clients with excellent care. We ensure that all of our drivers are pre- screened with background checks, proper licensing and certifications. We will continuing to take notes on the industry and its needs, always staying updated with HIPAA guidelines, ADA compliance, and Safety requirements, while keeping a keen eye on customer responsiveness, assuring quality transportation. We will do our part to provide professional, courteous and competent service at each delivery
