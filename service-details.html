WHO WE SERVE

Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT) is an important benefit for people who need assistance getting to and from medical appointments. At Natural Care Transportation, LLC., we provide (NEMT) services for Medicaid clients, including Children with Special Health Care Needs (CSHCN) and Transportation for Indigent Cancer Patients (TICP) and clients who have no other means of transportation.

HOW WILL WE SERVE

The primary objective of Natural Care Medical Transportation is to ensure that patients receive the appropriate care they need, without having to worry about the hassle of getting to and from medical appointments. This type of transportation is particularly important for those who require frequent medical attention, such as individuals undergoing chemotherapy or dialysis treatments. One of the major benefits of Natural Care Medical Transportation is the personalized care that is provided. Patients are typically transported in small groups or individually, ensuring that each patient receives the individual attention they require. In addition, the vehicles used for natural care medical transportation are equipped with the necessary medical equipment to ensure that patients are comfortable and safe during transport.

ONE MORE THING

Another benefit of Natural Care Medical Transportation is the peace of mind that it provides to both patients and their families. Patients can feel confident that they will arrive at their appointments on time and in a safe and comfortable manner. This can help to reduce anxiety and stress, which can be particularly important for patients who are dealing with chronic medical conditions. In addition to providing transportation to medical appointments, Natural Care Medical Transportation services can also provide transportation to other locations, such as grocery stores or pharmacies. This can be particularly important for individuals who are unable to drive or have limited mobility. Overall, Natural Care Medical Transportation is an important service that can provide peace of mind and reliable transportation for individuals who require non-emergency medical attention. By providing personalized care and reliable transportation, this service can help to improve the quality of life for patients and their families.
